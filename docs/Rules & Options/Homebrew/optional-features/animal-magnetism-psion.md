# Animal Magnetism

*Source: Psion* 

**Type:** Action

**Duration:** Up to 1 minute

**Range:** 5 ft

**PE Cost:** Special

For up to 1 minute, you have advantage on all Charisma checks directed at one creature of your choice that isn't hostile toward you, as with the spell friends.
