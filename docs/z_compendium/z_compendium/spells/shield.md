# Shield
*1st-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 reaction
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

An invisible barrier of magical force appears and protects you. Until the start of your next turn, you have a +5 bonus to AC, including against the triggering attack, and you take no damage from [magic missile](../../spells/magic-missile.md#).

**Classes**: [Artificer (Artillerist)](../../classes/artificer-artillerist-tce.md#), [Artificer (Battle Smith)](../../classes/artificer-battle-smith-tce.md#), [Fighter (Eldritch Knight)](../../classes/fighter-eldritch-knight.md#), [Rogue (Arcane Trickster)](../../classes/rogue-arcane-trickster.md#), [Sorcerer](../../classes/sorcerer.md#), [Warlock (The Hexblade)](../../classes/warlock-the-hexblade-xge.md#), [Wizard](../../classes/wizard.md#)

Player's Handbook p. 275. Available in the SRD and the Basic Rules.
