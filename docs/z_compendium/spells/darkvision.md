# Darkvision
*2nd-level, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, either a pinch of dried carrot or an agate
- **Duration:** 8 hour

You touch a willing creature to grant it the ability to see in the dark. For the duration, that creature has [darkvision](../../Rules%20&%20Options/5e%20Rules/senses.md##darkvision) out to a range of 60 feet.

**Classes**: [Artificer](../classes/artificer-tce.md#), [Druid](../classes/druid.md#), [Fighter (Eldritch Knight)](../classes/fighter-eldritch-knight.md#), [Monk (Way of Shadow)](../classes/monk-way-of-shadow.md#), [Ranger](../classes/ranger.md#), [Rogue (Arcane Trickster)](../classes/rogue-arcane-trickster.md#), [Sorcerer](../classes/sorcerer.md#), [Wizard](../classes/wizard.md#)

Player's Handbook p. 230. Available in the SRD.
